IoT-Based Smart Door

Secure, Automated, and Intelligent Access Control

Project Overview

The IoT-Based Smart Door System is a modern solution for home and office security. It integrates IoT technology to enable remote door control, real-time monitoring, and advanced authentication methods like RFID and face recognition.
